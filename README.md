# Articles

Article storage for [Graduate Texts in Technical Minecraft](https://beta.techmc.wiki).

## Got articles to submit?

See [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](https://github.com/gtmc-dev/articles?tab=contributing-ov-file).

## Pull Requests

Article submissions on the [website](https://beta.techmc.wiki) automatically open and sync with a PR here. The review and approval of an article submission are also performed by operating the corresponding PR.
